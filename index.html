About the KIND Lab

In the KIND lab, we collaborate on projects that aim to make accessibility mainstream in the software industry as well as in computing education. Additionally, we design software for people with disabilities to support their learning and access to information. In the KIND lab, we work remotely and meet in person when we can. We share virtual space and physical space to accommodate various work preferenes. We do our best to meet deadlines, but we prioritize your well being and mental health.
